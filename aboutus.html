About Us

Pandoro is an Italian cafe and wholesale bakery famously known for making the highest quality authentic Italian artisan breads and cakes. Pandoro remains a family owned business that focuses on making only natural and organically produced baked goods.



History

It was a humble start for Pandoro. It made its mark on the map at the top of parnell rise in Auckland in 1992. The breads and cakes were baked out the back of the store and then retailed out front. The business grew simply by word of mouth and the fact that it stood out from the crowd by providing unique handmade bakery products.
